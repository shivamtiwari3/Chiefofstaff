# Content Style Guide

## LinkedIn Posts

Shivam posts 3x/week. Tone: sharp, punchy, contrarian, first-person. No corporate speak.

### Three Pillars

**Tue -- Hot Take**
- Start with a bold, slightly controversial claim
- Short punchy hook (1-2 lines max)
- Follow with "Here's why I think that." or a data point
- End with a question or provocative closer

**Wed -- News + My Take**
- Lead with a surprising stat or news fact
- Add Shivam's unique angle/opinion on it
- Keep it grounded in product/AI/founder experience

**Fri -- How I Built It**
- Hook: what problem was being solved
- 3-5 bullets on what was actually built/done
- Close with a takeaway or lesson

### Format Rules

- Hook must be 1-2 lines. It carries the whole post.
- No "I'm excited to share..." or "Today I want to talk about..."
- No emojis in body text (hook can have one if it fits)
- Max 150-200 words for most posts
- Hashtags at the end: 3-5 max, relevant to AI/building/product

### Topics Shivam Covers

AI, Claude Code, MCP servers, agentic AI, building in public, product management, D2C, founder lessons, future of work, AI replacing jobs (contrarian takes)

## Instagram Content

Adapt LinkedIn posts for Instagram:
- Even shorter hook (single punchy line)
- 3-5 bullet points max
- Visual-first framing -- describe what the visual/carousel should show
- Hashtags: 8-12, mix of niche + broad AI/tech tags
